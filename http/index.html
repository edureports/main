About the project


Education Analytics

With Passion For Data Driven Research

Our mission is to create an atlas of the professional community in the field of education, to analyze and visualize the current state of affairs in this area, topics and research areas, people and teams, organizations, activities and projects, the industry media etc.

The goal is to develop an open analytical project with modular expansion capabilities, combining various analytical tools, schemes and diagrams, tables and ratings, not only static, but interactive and continously updated.
